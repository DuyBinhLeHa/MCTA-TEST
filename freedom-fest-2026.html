FREEDOM FEST 2026 – AMERICA 250

🎆 Celebrate 250 Years of American Independence!

Join the City of Morrow for Freedom Fest 2026 as we commemorate the 250th Anniversary of the United States of America. This milestone celebration honors our nation's rich history, enduring spirit of freedom, and the generations of Americans who have helped shape our country over the past 250 years.

Experience an unforgettable evening filled with live entertainment, delicious food vendors, family-friendly activities, community engagement, patriotic celebrations, and one of the area's most spectacular fireworks displays.

📅 Date: Saturday, June 27, 2026

⏰ Time: 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM

📍 Location: 1065 Olde Towne Morrow Rd, Morrow, GA 30260

As America celebrates its 250th anniversary, Freedom Fest 2026 invites residents and visitors to come together in a spirit of unity, gratitude, and pride. Enjoy live music, interactive activities, local vendors, and memorable experiences for all ages as we honor the past, celebrate the present, and inspire future generations.

🎇 The evening will conclude with a spectacular fireworks show celebrating 250 years of freedom, opportunity, and the American spirit.

✨ Everyone is welcome. Admission is FREE! ✨

Bring your lawn chairs, picnic blankets, family, friends, and patriotic spirit as we celebrate America 250 together in the City of Morrow.
